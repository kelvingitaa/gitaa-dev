How long does a website take to build?+
For the Launch Package (5 pages), we deliver in 14 days. The Growth Package takes 21–28 days. Pro/Custom timelines depend on scope — we'll give you a precise timeline on the discovery call.
Do you work with businesses outside Nairobi?+
Absolutely. We work across Kenya — Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru — and internationally. Everything is handled remotely via video calls, Figma, and WhatsApp.
What do I need to provide to get started?+
Your business details, goals, any existing branding (logo, colours), and content (text + photos). If you don't have content ready, we can help. We'll walk you through everything on the kickoff call.
Can I update the site myself after launch?+
Yes — that's a priority for us. Growth Package clients get a full training session. We build on Webflow or with a CMS so you can update text, images, blog posts, and listings without touching code.
Do you offer payment plans?+
Yes. We work on 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For larger Pro projects, we can arrange a 3-stage payment plan. We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, and international payments via PesaPal.
What's included in post-launch support?+
Launch Package: 14 days of bug fixes and small tweaks. Growth Package: 30 days. Pro clients: 60 days of ongoing optimization — we monitor performance and make data-driven improvements.
